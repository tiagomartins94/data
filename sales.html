In this project I created 4 csv files:

  • customer (customer) - 609 customer data (tax number, name, date of birth)
  • employees (employees) - 10 employee data (name, employee number)
  • product(product) - 33 product data (SKU, denomination, price, product category)
  • sales(sales) - 1042 sales data (day of sale, customer tax number, product SKU, employee number)

With this data, I created a dashboard in which I provide some information about billing, the best customer, the best employee and the best-selling product.
